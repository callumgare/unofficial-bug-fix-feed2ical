Feed2iCal

  • View and/or Subscribe to RSS/RDF/Atom Feeds in iCalendar (vCal 2.0) format.
  • Subscriptions have been tested with Apple's iCal application and Microsoft Outlook.
  • Once you have subscribed to a feed, iTunes or iCloud can be used to synchronize it with your iPhone, iPad, or iPod.
